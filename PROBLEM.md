# Passive Mental Health Crisis Detection from Daily Digital Behavior

**Domain:** Mental Health

**Complexity:** 5/5

**Description:**

Suicide and severe mental health crises are often preceded by weeks of detectable behavioral changes that go unnoticed. Build a privacy-first, on-device AI model that passively monitors anonymized smartphone behavioral signals — typing rhythm, app usage patterns, response latency, and sleep schedule inferred from screen-on/off events — to generate a real-time mental wellness index. When the index crosses a clinician-defined risk threshold, the system triggers a soft-touch check-in notification and, upon consent, connects the user with a peer counselor or crisis line. All processing must occur on-device with zero raw data transmitted to servers.
